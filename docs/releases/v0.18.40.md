## Verdant Vine Coil

Verdant Hollow gains the **Vine Coil**, a thorned living seed that keeps a
compact orbit around the champion and releases a slow pair of opposite shots.
It is a clear early lane-change lesson rather than a bullet-flood: 10 HP, one
damage, a 124-frame cadence, and only 8% of the stage's existing Flutterbat
weight. The pool remains exactly 100, so the milestone does not add enemy
bodies, alter seeded draw count, or inflate early-game attrition.

The Coil has a dedicated 8×8 silhouette in the already phase-safe Verdant OBJ
slot. Stage boundaries now refresh phase-safe combat identity after loading
stage boss/miniboss art, which prevents a prior stage's multiplexed specialist
tile from persisting into the new dungeon's first room. Live-ROM identity
coverage exercises the real transition and compares the resulting OBJ VRAM
tile against the generated Vine Coil art.

PACK now identifies the outdoor connector as **RIFTWILD** and names its next
destination (for example, **NEXT EMBER DEPTHS**) instead of describing that
screen as an ordinary numbered stage. Towns likewise identify themselves as a
**VILLAGE / SAFE HAVEN**.

The cartridge remains 128 KiB / eight banks, with 1,209 bytes of home-bank
headroom and 1,260 bytes in its tightest switchable bank.
