## Ember Rift Armor

Rift Armor now begins with Ember Depths (boss three). From that stage onward,
each individual hit on a Colossus is capped at three damage. Crystal and
Verdant bosses retain full damage as early pattern lessons.

Matched clean-save Picsean samples preserve all three first-boss clears and
raise the Ember Colossus median clear from 560 to 882 frames (about 9.3 to
14.7 seconds), with no deaths or controller stalls.

This release also narrows the build dependency for the title-only version
header: routine version bumps now rebuild `title.o` plus the final link rather
than the entire C tree. The 128 KiB cartridge layout and gameplay budget are
unchanged.
