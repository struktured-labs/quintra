# Quintra v0.18.66 — Objective Wings

Dungeon scale now comes from geography as well as room count. Every stage's
first junction branches around a large Sigil/Warden/Waystone objective wing
and into the deeper expedition, then rejoins at room ten. The required route
spans 19–29 room-to-room moves without adding filler screens or forcing a
cleared-wing refight.

SELECT renders the new reciprocal junction directly. At room one, the current
cyan square has two hollow frontier squares and two visible links: east toward
the violet Sigil wing and south toward the deeper dungeon. The same graph owns
the physical doors, the controller route, and the 6×5 pocket map.

The cartridge still contains 20–30 one-screen dungeon fields, so this does not
claim to be the final continuous-world solution. Larger scrolling outdoor
fields and genuinely scrolling Penta Dragon-style Colossus arenas remain
active engine work.

Player collision now samples the centre of the champion as well as both
corners. A one-tile pillar can no longer slip between the old corner probes
when approached through its lower edge, and the nine-stage controller proof
no longer wedges in the resulting top-wall pocket. If enemy knockback begins
with the body already overlapping scenery, controlled depenetration remains
possible until the hero reaches open floor.

This release refreshes the ROM-bound native mGBA curriculum and timed
checkpoints against the new graph. The full build, topology, Compass, door,
boss-threshold, giant-boss, cartridge, suspend, replay, and clean-rebuild gates
must pass before publication.
