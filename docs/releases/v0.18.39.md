## Authored Pounce Speed

`charge_speed` is now an actual gameplay parameter rather than unused content
metadata. Standard 96-speed chargers (Rope and Frost Lancer) retain their
readable two-pixel lane dash. The Bog Toad's authored 120-speed pounce moves
three pixels per frame, giving Toxic Mire a genuinely distinct telegraphed
threat without adding enemy bodies, damage, projectiles, or RNG draws.

Live-ROM checks cover the fast Bog Toad pounce and the unchanged Frost Lancer
cadence, together with enemy density and door/sprite visibility. The new build
remains a 128 KiB / eight-bank GBC cartridge with 1,239 bytes of home-bank and
1,290 bytes of its tightest switchable-bank headroom.
