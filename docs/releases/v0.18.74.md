# Quintra v0.18.74 — Tester’s Edge

Easy is now a more reliable deep-boss inspection mode. It preserves the exact
generated world, Colossus HP, movement, projectile patterns, and World
Collapse used by Normal, but the strengthened tester kit can deal up to five
damage through late Rift Armor instead of Normal's authored three-damage cap.

The paired one-minute boss curriculum improves from 34/45 Easy clears to
42/45. All five champions now clear the Void Lord fixture, and the other three
uncleared policies remain alive at timeout. Normal is byte-for-byte unchanged
at the combat decision: its late-boss durability and incoming damage remain
the canonical balance target.

The release regenerates all 460 PyBoy and 460 native mGBA checkpoints against
the final ROM. It also records the Penta Dragon scale comparison: Quintra's
128×80 Void Lord matches the reference's roughly four-fifths-screen width,
while Crystal retains the campaign's true camera-travelling arena.
