## Wolfkin Fang Forms

Wolfkin no longer fires the ambiguous travelling slash as their base weapon.
Pressing **A with a direction** performs a narrow adjacent **Fang Stab**;
pressing **A without a direction** makes one wider **Fang Sweep** hitbox, so
bosses cannot be hit by a stack of overlapping pseudo-projectiles.

Holding A commits the champion to **Max Strike**: a short, invulnerable lane
dash with a visible spear-like hit. Its four-segment HUD bar is full when
ready and refills across a 150-frame cooldown. Boss health and shop offers
retain priority over that contextual HUD lane, and weapon swaps retain their
own authored A behaviour.

The cartridge remains 128 KiB / eight banks. Home-bank headroom is 1,177
bytes; the tightest switchable bank retains 1,260 bytes.
