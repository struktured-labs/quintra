# Quintra v0.18.69 — Riftwild Unbound

Riftwild no longer consists only of fixed 160px rooms. Every one of its
sixteen logical cells is now a 224×136 scrolling world behind the Game Boy
Color's 160×136 playfield viewport. Crossing the former right wall pans into
64px of additional generated terrain; the true eastern graph threshold now
lives at x=216.

The far field has first-class terrain, not decorative overscan. It owns a
paired seed-rotated landmark, trees, paths, collision, and enemy pressure in a
WRAM strip shared by rendering, player and projectile collision, enemy
movement, automated routing, and cartridge tests. East-to-west arrivals begin
at the 64px camera bound with the champion already visible. The complete 4×4
Riftwild grows from 640×544 logical pixels to 896×544 before counting its
nonlinear Rift Wells.

This reduces the cadence of room loads while preserving procedural fixtures
and learnable geography. Wide-to-wide boundaries use a short blanked rebuild
because the source consumes 28 of the hardware background map's 32 columns;
ordinary dungeon rooms retain their Zelda-style slide transitions. It is an
incremental engine milestone toward larger no-load outdoor regions, not a
claim that the final seamless overworld is finished.

Live-ROM coverage traverses all sixteen fields, verifies both landmark halves
and the obsolete seam, exercises both camera bounds and reciprocal graph
arrivals, confirms Rift Well recovery persistence, and completes the route
from the opening boss through Riftwild to the next dungeon. The colossal
Crystal arena and dense-frame performance contracts remain green, and all 370
external PyBoy checkpoints are regenerated against this ROM.
