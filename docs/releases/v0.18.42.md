## Revealed Cache Puzzles

Pressure plates now produce an actual Zelda-style secret: stepping on the
plate, or sliding its nearby 2×2 cairn onto it, clears the marker and reveals
a two-tile north-wall side passage. That passage enters the existing generated
secret cache flow rather than paying out an arbitrary loose pickup. The
authored x=7/x=12 puzzle locations place this opening beside, never over, the
centered progression door.

The solve has a distinct discovery jingle and the cairn uses the wall palette,
so it reads as old landscape until the player learns its use. Live-ROM
regression coverage now moves a champion onto the plate and verifies the
marker is consumed and both wall cells become a usable door.

The title release check also proves row 15 is blank. Best score is available
through **SELECT → Records** only; it cannot leave a bare persistent number in
the title's lore tableau.

The cartridge remains 128 KiB / eight banks, with 1,150 bytes of home-bank
headroom and 1,260 bytes in its tightest switchable bank.
