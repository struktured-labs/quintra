## First Colossus Pacing

The opening Crystal Colossus now has 200 HP, up from 160. Its damage,
projectiles, body contact, rewards, and all later boss progression are
unchanged. The goal is simply to ensure an early shop/relic build sees the
opening pattern, ring, body-spacing, and phase break before winning.

Matched clean-save Picsean samples retain all three first-boss clears while
raising the median clear from 432 to 558 frames (about 7.2 to 9.3 seconds).

Verified on the 128 KiB CGB ROM: typed content tests, live boss identity,
title/version, cartridge/header and budget checks, 26/26 gameplay smoke
checks, SRAM/reproducibility/media preflight, and refreshed release captures.
